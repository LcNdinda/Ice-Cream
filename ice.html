HOW IT'S MADE

  • Cut off the tops of the strawberries.
  • Pour the cut strawberries into a blender.
  • Add filtered water into blender.
  • Blend until mixture has a smoothie-like consistency.
  • Pour blended creation into pop molds and insert sticks at the center of each mold.
  • Freeze molds for an hour or until frozen.
